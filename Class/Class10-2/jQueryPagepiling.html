Fried Eggs

Who does'nt love the smell of friend eggs with bacon in the morning? Well, probably vegans now that I think about it. Still, for many of us omnivorous food lovers, few things can top a hearty couple of fried eggs. Wether it is for breakfast of afternoon snack, this recipe can help you get the most out of your fried eggs.

What you will need

  • eggs1
  • frying pan
  • oil
  • Salt
  • pepper

Step 1

Opening an egg

Gently but firmly grasp the egg. Rap it against the countertop, so its side lands squarely against the surface. It’s better to give it one sharp tap than several gentle, tentative cracks. Multiple hits can increase the risk of shattering the shell into lots of small pieces. You ideally want one larger split. Smashing the egg too hard can crush the shell, making it nearly inevitable that you’ll wind up with shell in your egg.

Step 2

Put oil in pan

Use frying oil of your choice in a non stick pan for best result, a medium amount will ensure that the egg is cooked throughout.

Step 3

Put egg in pan

Very carefully place your eggs in the pan. Avoid too high a distance as this can break the eggs. Keep stove at a medium temperature too avoid burning the eggs.

Step 4

Cover pan with lid

Cover pan and leave cooking for some time. By closing the pan with the lid, the water vapor will slowly cook the egg to the desired level.

  • Final Step!!!

    • Step 5

    Serve the Eggs!

    You are done! Carefully slide the eggs and serve it with any desired side. Whether you are having a hearty breakfast, or an afternoon snack, you are about to have an amazing snack.
