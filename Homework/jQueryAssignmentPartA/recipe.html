Step 1

Opening an egg

Gently but firmly grasp the egg. Rap it against the countertop, so its side lands squarely against the surface. It’s better to give it one sharp tap than several gentle, tentative cracks. Multiple hits can increase the risk of shattering the shell into lots of small pieces. You ideally want one larger split. Smashing the egg too hard can crush the shell, making it nearly inevitable that you’ll wind up with shell in your egg.
